Ingredients:
 - 400gms finely chopped chicken
 - 800gms basmati rice
 - 1 1/2 teaspoons garam masala
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 2 eggs
 - 1/2 cup vegetable oil
 - 1 teaspoon haldi
 - Condiments as per taste 

Directions:
 1. Cook the chicken in a bowl of water for about 20 mins.
 
 2. Cook the rice along with the spices and condiments in a large vessel till it turns slightly yellow. Add the oil and continue to stir.
 
 3. Add the chicken and continue the cooking for the next 30 mins. Serve hot.
